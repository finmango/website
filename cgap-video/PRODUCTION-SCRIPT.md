## Captions (recommended for the whole video)

Reviewers often watch muted. Style: **DM Sans Medium, white text on an
80%-black bar, bottom-center, max 2 lines.** Use the paragraphs above
verbatim — they're the cleaned script (FinMango, CGAP, IMF all spelled
right; auto-captions will mangle them). During 0:03–0:08, keep captions
above the name card, not over it.

## Design cheatsheet (for anything you add)

- Fonts: **DM Sans Bold** (headlines/numbers) · **JetBrains Mono**
  (labels, uppercase, wide letter-spacing) — both available in Canva
- Colors: cream `#FAF7F2` · ink `#1A1A1A` · orange `#FF6B35`
  (orange only for numbers and thin accent bars, never as a background)
- No drop shadows, no gradients. Thin 1px rules. Flat and editorial.
