Graphic: `eduardo-charles-alba-quote-square.png` / `-vertical.png`

### LinkedIn
Eduardo Charles Alba spent ten years inside Latin America's financial
inclusion movement. The headline goal across the region was always the same:
get more people into the system. Open more accounts. Issue more cards. Move
more money through the formal economy.

And yet, in the data, he kept finding something the headline missed.
Inclusion without trust, without products designed for real lives, without
honest measurement of outcomes, can leave people worse off than before.

As a 2026 FinMango Global Ambassador, Eduardo is bringing regulators,
banks, and partners together to shift the regional focus from access to
actual outcomes. He's also helping FinMango develop a Financial Health
Predictor and connecting that work with the American Statistical Association
to launch data challenges that change how financial health gets measured.
