# 2026 Ambassador Announcement — LinkedIn Caption Templates

Three templates to copy, paste, and make your own. Swap the bracketed bits
for your details. Riff on them; don't read them verbatim. Voice: real,
not rehearsed — no "humbled and honored," no corporate-speak, no em dashes.

That feeling is what pulled me toward financial health work, and it's why I'm joining FinMango's 2026 Ambassador cohort.

Over the next year I'll be [what you plan to do — e.g., running workshops at my campus, building a resource guide for first-gen students, translating personal finance into plain language for my community]. I don't have all the answers. I have a lot of questions, a lot of energy, and 10 teammates across [cities/countries] who care about the same thing.

If your community needs this work too, send me a note. I'd love to compare notes.
