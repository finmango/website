# 2026 FinMango Ambassador Announcements — Per-Ambassador Captions

Captions for FinMango's official announcements of each 2026 Global Ambassador.
Pair each with the matching graphic in this folder.

**Voice rules** (same as the ambassador template file):
real, not rehearsed. No "humbled and honored." No corporate-speak. No em dashes.

**Graphics:**
- Square (1080×1080) → LinkedIn feed, Instagram feed, X
- Vertical (1080×1920) → Instagram/LinkedIn Stories, Reels covers

**Tag (when ambassadors share):** @FinMango on LinkedIn and Instagram, @finmangoapp on X.
**Hashtags:** `#FinMangoAmbassador` `#FinancialHealth`
**CTA link:** finmango.org/ambassadors

**Suggested cadence:** one ambassador per day for 11 days, or a group announcement
post on day 1 followed by individual spotlights. Mix in the order so countries
and stories alternate.
