why join

More Than a Title on Your Résumé.

🌍
Global Network
Join a community of young leaders from 15+ countries who share your commitment to financial health equity.
🎓
Training & Mentorship
Receive guidance in financial health advocacy, public engagement, and leadership from experienced practitioners.
🛠️
Real-World Impact
Design and lead projects in your own community — locally or digitally — with FinMango's support and resources.
🤝
Completely Free
No cost, ever. We believe financial barriers shouldn't stand between passionate young adults and leadership opportunities.
🥭 Ambassador deliverable
Submit a Money Mango cascade from your country.
Share a real-world money chain reaction — payday lenders, healthcare debt, gig work, evictions, benefit cliffs — from where you live. FinMango Research reviews every submission. Accepted cascades go live in today's daily puzzle with credit to you.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Young adults aged 18–34 with a global perspective are eligible. The program welcomes applicants worldwide — the 2024 cohort represented seven countries with an average age of 24. The program is competitive with 300+ applicants annually.
The program runs annually with flexible scheduling. Most ambassadors contribute 10–30 hours during their term, with optional check-ins for ongoing support. You choose when and how to implement projects.
Absolutely. Ambassadors work within their own communities and connect globally. Projects can be conducted locally or digitally — you have complete flexibility in how you make a difference.
The program is completely FREE. We believe in removing financial barriers to leadership opportunities and want this accessible to passionate young adults worldwide.
Reach out to global@finmango.org — we're happy to help.
