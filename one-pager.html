The affordability crisis is a mobility crisis. FinMango uses data and technology to fix the systemic barriers — housing, healthcare, debt, and predatory lending — crushing the financial health of young adults and vulnerable communities.

🥭 What We Build

  • Free financial tools — calculators, simulators & games

  • Barrier Breakers — youth innovation challenge
  • Ambassador Program — a global advocacy network

📊 The Problem We're Solving

  • 63% of Americans can't cover a $500 emergency
  • 56% of young adults report financial stress harming mental health
  • 31% still believe the American Dream holds true
  • The affordability crisis is, at its core, a mobility crisis
