Barrier Breakers Judging Rubric

Demo Day (Oral Presentations) & Innovation Gallery (Poster Presentations) Scoring Criteria

This rubric is used to evaluate all projects on March 20, 2026. The same criteria apply to Demo Day presentations and Innovation Gallery posters. Each criterion is scored 1-5, for a maximum of 100 points.

Demo Day & Innovation Gallery 100 POINTS TOTAL

Criterion Points
Problem Understanding
Depth of research on the financial health barrier. Does the team understand why this problem exists, who it affects, and its root causes?
 25
Solution Quality & Innovation
How creative, feasible, and thoughtful is the proposed solution? Could it realistically be implemented? Does it address the root cause?
 25
Presentation & Communication
Clarity, engagement, and professionalism of the presentation. Can the team communicate their idea compellingly and answer questions effectively?
 25
Impact Potential
Could this solution realistically help young adults overcome financial barriers? What is the potential scale and long-term impact?
 25
TOTAL 100

Demo Day Format (Oral Presentations)

Component Time
Pitch Presentation
Present your problem, research, solution, and impact. Slides optional but recommended.
 5 min
Judge Q&A
Answer questions from the judging panel about your solution, research, and team.
 2 min

Innovation Gallery Format (Poster Presentations)

Component Details
Poster Display
Present your project on a poster or display board. Judges will rotate through all stations.
 Provided
Judge Interaction
Explain your project when judges visit. Be prepared for questions about your research and solution.
 ~5 min
