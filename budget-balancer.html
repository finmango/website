The 50/30/20 Rule

A simple framework for financial stability.

50% Needs

Essentials

Housing, groceries, utilities, and transportation. These are the bills you must pay to survive and work.

30% Wants

Lifestyle

Dining out, hobbies, subscriptions, and travel. This is the fun stuff that makes life enjoyable.

20% Savings

Future You

Emergency fund, retirement investing, and debt repayment. Pay yourself first!
