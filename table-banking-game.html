Interactive Simulator · 5 Languages · Your Currency

A village bank runs on trust — not credit scores.

Join eight neighbors around a shared table. Over six monthly meetings, you'll contribute to a common pool, vote on who gets loans, weather emergencies, and build (or break) trust with the group. Kenya calls it a chama, Mexico a tanda, Ghana a susu, South Africa a stokvel. Hundreds of millions of people bank this way — especially where banks won't go.
