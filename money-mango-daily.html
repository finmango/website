Why a puzzle a day?

Financial health isn't a literacy problem.

It's a system problem. Money Mango makes that system visible — one decision at a time — and points you to the people working to change it.

01 / Daily

See the system.

Each puzzle is a real money moment the system didn't make easy — banking deserts, benefit cliffs, predatory lending, eviction records. The kind of thing 'just budget better' won't fix.

02 / Honest

Name the cost.

Every reveal shows the structural math — the wage theft, the payday spread, the cliff that punishes a $200 raise. So you can see what's being taken, not just what to optimize.

03 / Sticky

Move the system.

Every puzzle ends with a way to act — research to read, policy to support, work FinMango is doing on the ground. Mobility is built individually AND collectively. We do both.

Mission

Money decisions shouldn't be a privilege.

Money Mango Daily exists because financial health is a right, not a privilege — and the system that decides who gets it doesn't change on its own. FinMango is a 501(c)(3) building economic mobility infrastructure for young adults and vulnerable communities. Donors keep this work going.
