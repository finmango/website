Why a puzzle, not a quiz

Real money systems are hidden in plain sight.

A multiple-choice quiz tests whether you know the right answer. A puzzle makes you find the pattern — which is what financial health actually looks like in real life.

01 / Pattern

The pattern is the lesson.

Each group is a category like "Should build credit but doesn't" or "The hidden poverty tax." You don't learn it by reading — you learn it by seeing four things share a category you didn't expect.

02 / Systemic

Not literacy — structure.

Every puzzle is built around real systems: banking deserts, benefit cliffs, eviction records, wage theft, lending discrimination. The "trap" tiles are the ones the system hides.

03 / Action

Each solve links to the work.

Every group reveals a one-line truth and a path forward — research, policy, programs, or tools FinMango is building. Mobility is individual AND collective. We do both.
