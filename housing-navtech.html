What's Next

From diagnosis to done.

01
The navigation layer. Eviction prevention · Rental assistance · Path to ownership
+

Today our tools tell a family where they stand. Phase 01 turns that into the specific next action.

  • Eviction PreventionLegal aid, rental assistance, and mediation — matched to your county.
  • Rental Assistance RouterEvery program you qualify for, with deadlines tracked.
  • Path to OwnershipDown-payment assistance and first-time-buyer programs.
  • Utility & Arrears HelpLIHEAP and back-rent relief before the shutoff.
02
Now Building Frontline-worker dashboard. One workflow for housing counselors & benefits navigators
+

30 minutes of paperwork. Becomes 3 minutes of routing.

  • Caseload DashboardEvery household, every barrier, in one view.
  • AI Intake → ActionA 5-minute intake becomes a ranked action plan.
  • Verified Program DatabaseReal local housing resources. Geo-matched.
  • Follow-up & StatusSubmitted applications and what comes next.
03
Feedback loop to policy. Every navigation becomes signal
+

Aggregated and anonymized, each navigation becomes a real-time housing-stress signal.

  • Hotspot AlertsEviction waves and rent spikes, as they emerge.
  • Policy DashboardWhere the housing system is failing, in real time.
  • Research APIOpen, aggregate housing-stress data.
  • Community BriefingsAuto-generated, hyperlocal.
