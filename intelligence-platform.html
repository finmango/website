A signal in the wild

One factory closure.
A 90-day cascade.

How it works

Four AI loops. Always running.

The platform doesn't wait for a crisis. It watches, reasons, acts, and closes the loop by guiding citizens directly to enrollment.

01
Detect

Spot distress before it's reported

Monitoring agents ingest cross-domain data 24/7. When signals deviate from local baselines, the system flags emerging stress at the zip-code level — weeks ahead of official reporting.

BLS · Census · HUD FRED · JCHS · Trends
02
Inform

Empower decision-makers instantly

Generate tailored Community Health Briefs for every agency. Risk scores, trend analysis, and actionable policy recommendations on demand.

Weekly briefs On-demand queries
03
Respond

Deploy resources at the speed of need

Automatically trigger targeted outreach and eligibility pre-screening across relevant programs, natively through existing government channels.

SMS · mail · IVR Caseworker assist
04
Enroll

Guide citizens through the red tape

An AI enrollment agent walks citizens through multi-program applications — SNAP, Medicaid, housing — in a single, streamlined session by pre-filling known data.
