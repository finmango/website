Our Founder

Bob Gillingham

Bob Gillingham

Lead Instructor & Board Member

Bob Gillingham, a 1969 graduate of the University of Illinois, has built an impressive legacy as both a successful auto dealership owner and an influential educator. His journey into education began in 1997 when he started teaching Junior Achievement to high school students, focusing on business startup principles.

By 2003, he had refined his curriculum to teach students how to invest in the stock market‚Äîa topic that has since become his primary focus. Bob has expanded his reach with FinMango, teaching approximately 8,000 students annually across 27 high schools and colleges in five different states.
