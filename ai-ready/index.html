01

This is a financial health issue, not just a tech issue.

When people talk about AI, they focus on the technology. We focus on the money part. Because job displacement, income volatility, retraining costs, and investment uncertainty are all financial health problems.

Job Displacement

Entire categories of work are being automated. Not just factory jobs. Customer service, data entry, legal research, even creative work.

Income Volatility

Gig work is growing. Full-time positions are being restructured. More people are cobbling together income from multiple unstable sources.

Reskilling Costs

Learning new skills takes time and money. Many people can't afford to stop earning while they retrain. That's a financial planning problem.

Investment Uncertainty

Which industries will grow? Which will shrink? Your career, your retirement savings, and your side projects all depend on navigating this well.

03 who we are

Built by people who've been doing this for years.

FinMango is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on financial health. Not financial literacy (there's a difference). We believe your financial situation is shaped by systems, not just personal choices, and we build tools that meet you where you are.

We've partnered with the World Health Organization, World Bank, and Google Health. Our resources have reached over 10 million people across 100+ countries. This AI-Ready initiative is our latest effort to get ahead of a financial health crisis before it hits.
