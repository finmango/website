Executive Board Member & Lead Instructor

Bob Gillingham

A 1969 graduate of the University of Illinois, Bob Gillingham has built an impressive legacy as both a successful auto dealership owner and an influential educator. Teaching approximately 8,000 students annually across 27 high schools and colleges in five states, Bob's dedication to financial education has earned him recognition as the 2024 Northeast Region Ohio School Boards Association Volunteer of the Year and Time Magazine's Dealer of the Year for Educational Impact.
