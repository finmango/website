Barrier Breakers is co-hosted with Kent State University, combining FinMango's mission of financial health equity with KSU's commitment to transformative education and community impact.

📍 Kent State University Campus — Kent, Ohio

Why Kent State?

Kent State University has been an essential partner since the very first Barrier Breakers event. Their campus provides a world-class venue for students to present, compete, and connect with mentors—bringing the energy and resources of a major research university to high schoolers solving real financial health challenges.

From providing event space and AV support to connecting students with KSU faculty and programs, this partnership gives competitors access to opportunities they wouldn't have otherwise.

How It Works

Your Path to Competition

Mar 4
Registration Closed

Teams have been formed and registered. Registration is now closed.

Early Mar
Build Your Solution

All teams notified. Research your barrier and build a solution—use AI tools like Gemini or Claude to prototype. Don't just start—finish your project! Even a rough prototype is better than just an idea.

Mar 20
Present & Compete

Present at Kent State in Demo Day (oral) or Innovation Gallery (poster). Cash prizes + scholarships!

How selection works: Top project from each track presents on the Demo Day stage. Everyone else competes in the Innovation Gallery. All teams can win cash prizes. Everyone who applies competes!

Prizes & Awards

🚀 Demo Day (Oral Presentations)

Top team from each of 5 tracks. 5-min pitch + 2-min Q&A.

  • 1st Place — $7,500 ($4,000 cash + $2,500 scholarship + $1,000 school)
  • 2nd Place — $4,650 ($2,500 cash + $1,500 scholarship + $650 school)
  • 3rd Place — $2,750 ($1,250 cash + $1,000 scholarship + $500 school)
  • 4th Place — $1,850 ($750 cash + $750 scholarship + $350 school)
  • 5th Place — $1,250 ($500 cash + $500 scholarship + $250 school)

Cash prizes & scholarships split among team members. Scholarships require attending KSU & selecting ACCBE major/minor. School grants go directly to the school.

📊 Innovation Gallery (Poster Presentations)

All teams not in Demo Day. Present 1-on-1 with judges via poster or display.

  • 1st Place$1,250 Cash
  • 2nd Place$750 Cash
  • 3rd Place$250 Cash

🧠 Knowledge Track

Test your financial literacy! Open to all attendees. 20-30 min phone test.

  • 1st Place$250 Cash
  • 2nd Place$150 Cash
  • 3rd Place$100 Cash

Student cash prizes split among team members. High school awards go directly to the school. Prize amounts may increase as we add more sponsors.

Demo Day Judges

Meet the Judges

Our judges bring diverse expertise across finance, entrepreneurship, and community impact to evaluate Demo Day presentations.

Cass Lacey
Early Talent Program Manager
 
Financial health matters to me because it helps create stability and security for families. Having a strong financial foundation means being prepared for the unexpected and knowing the people I care about are protected. It also allows us to plan with confidence and focus on what matters most — time together and long-term well-being.
Mia Fawls
Associate Financial Planner
 
Financial health knowledge matters to me because many people tend to focus only on the present and don't always realize how important it is to prepare for the future. Understanding concepts like saving, managing debt, and planning for retirement early in life can set people up for long-term success. When your financial health is strong, it can create more opportunities and flexibility in both your career and personal life.
Drew Mallott
SVP, Platform Strategy at Bank of America
KSU Grad '04 (Marketing)
While long-term financial health is a series of individual choices, collectively we can all learn more over time and consistently improve. The earlier positive financial health habits are learned and put into practice, the greater the long-term impact, which is why the work done by FinMango is critical.
Peyton Zachrich
Owner, Evolve Egress & Exteriors
Entrepreneur & Real Estate Investor
Financial health matters to me because it can lead to greater agency and freedom in people's lives.
Ryan Bayonnet, CFP®
Founder, Hyland Wealth Management
Certified Financial Planner® · Fee-Only RIA
Financial health matters to me because I've seen firsthand how the right guidance at the right time can transform a family's future. Everyone deserves access to honest, transparent financial advice — not just the wealthy.
Ashley Wasielewski
VP, Community Relations Manager – Cleveland, Indianapolis & Wisconsin Markets
Bank of America
I really believe it is important to learn financial literacy and learn it ASAP. I still remember our program in elementary school. I want to give that experience to others and help them avoid costly mistakes.
