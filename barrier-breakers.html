The problem

Financial health isn't about knowing more. It's about facing fewer walls.

The barriers are systemic: transportation that costs more than the job pays, rent that eats 60% of a paycheck, medical debt from a single ER visit. These aren't problems you budget your way out of. They're problems you have to build your way through.

How it works

Three steps. No fluff.

01
Pick a barrier
Choose a track: transportation, housing, healthcare, financial literacy, or bring your own. Research why it exists and who it hurts most.
02
Build a solution
Policy proposal, app prototype, community tool, financial literacy campaign. Use AI tools. Prototype fast. Make it real.
03
Present and compete
Demo Day for oral presentations. Innovation Gallery for posters. Knowledge Track for financial literacy. Everyone who applies competes.
