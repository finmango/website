In Partnership with Kent State University

Barrier Breakers is proudly hosted in collaboration with the Ambassador Crawford College of Business and Entrepreneurship at Kent State University.

This partnership brings together academic excellence, entrepreneurial spirit, and real-world problem-solving to empower high school students to tackle the financial health barriers affecting their communities.

Compete on Kent State's campus, connect with university mentors, and gain exposure to college-level innovation and business thinking.
