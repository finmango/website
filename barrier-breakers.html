Innovation Tracks

Choose one challenge. Research why it's a barrier to financial health. Build a solutionpolicy proposal, app prototype, financial literacy tool, or anything else that could help young adults break free from these barriers.

Transportation

Application Review

What We Look For When You Apply

Like Y Combinator, we care about potential and ability to executenot just the idea.

Team & Execution Ability

Why is your team suited to tackle this barrier? What makes you capable of building a real solution?

Problem Clarity

How well do you understand the barrier you're addressing? Have you done your research?

Solution Potential

Is your initial approach thoughtful and feasible? Does it actually address the root cause?

Ambition & Impact

How big could this be if it works? Could it help thousands of young adults?

Event Day Judging

How Projects Are Scored

Same rubric for Demo Day and Innovation Gallery. Judges score each criterion 1-5.

Problem Understanding

25 pts

Depth of research on the financial health barrier. Do you understand why this problem exists and who it affects?

Solution Quality

25 pts

Innovation, feasibility, and thoughtfulness of your approach. Could this actually work in the real world?

Presentation

25 pts

Clarity, engagement, and professionalism. Can you communicate your idea compellingly?

Impact Potential

25 pts

Could this realistically help young adults overcome financial barriers? What's the potential scale?
