How It Works

Our analyzer uses established food access research methodologies to estimate food desert risk in your area.

üè™

Grocery Store Access

Measures proximity to supermarkets and large grocery stores. Low-income areas more than 1 mile (urban) or 10 miles (rural) from a store are considered low access.

ü•ó

Fresh Food Availability

Evaluates the presence of stores selling fresh produce, lean proteins, and whole grains. Areas with only convenience stores or fast food score lower.

Transportation Access

Considers public transit availability and vehicle access rates. Limited transportation compounds food access challenges significantly.

Note: This tool provides an educational estimate based on zip code characteristics. For official food desert data, visit the USDA Food Access Research Atlas.
