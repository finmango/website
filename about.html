About FinMango
Since 2017

Making financial health a right, not a privilege.

The affordability crisis is the defining financial challenge of our generation. We use data, technology, and education to fix what's actually broken.

Meet the team How we work
Our mission

Financial health is a systems problem. We treat it that way.

Solving complex problems that endanger the financial health of young adults and vulnerable communities.

How we work

What we do.

01 · Diagnose

Research

We pinpoint the systemic barriers — housing, healthcare, debt, predatory lending — that make financial health impossible, building the evidence base policymakers need to act.

Learn more
02 · Equip

Education

We reach students while they're still financially healthy — before the loans, before the credit cards. Our programs work across income levels, from suburbs to inner-city schools.

Learn more
03 · Change

Advocacy

Education alone won't fix a broken system. Our global ambassador network drives policy change on the structural barriers budgeting advice can't solve.

Learn more
Who we are

Our values.

Globally Inclusive

We work to inspire coalitions and are, in turn, deeply inspired by those who share our passion for change.

Insatiably Curious

We never stop learning. We deploy new knowledge to increase our capacity and explore what might be possible.

Boldly Optimistic

We pursue a better life for all with boundless passion and unwavering belief in positive transformation.

Fanatically Focused

We choose our work and partners intentionally based on research, data and capacity with an eye for impact.

Honest & Transparent

We are humble enough to recognize our shortcomings and brave enough to shine light on system failures.
