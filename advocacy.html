FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?
Young adults aged 18-29 with a global perspective are eligible to apply. The program welcomes applicants from around the worldâ€”for reference, the 2024 cohort represented seven different countries with an average age of 24. The program is competitive with 300+ applicants annually.
How much time does it require?
The program runs annually with flexible scheduling to accommodate your commitments. You can choose when to implement projects throughout the year. Most ambassadors spend 10-30 hours contributing to FinMango during their term, with optional regular check-ins for ongoing support.
Do I need to work locally or can projects be online?
Ambassadors work within their own communities and connect globally through various platforms. Projects can be conducted locally or digitally, allowing for both in-person and online impact. You have complete flexibility in how you choose to make a difference.
What are the benefits of joining?
The program offers a unique opportunity to champion financial health as a fundamental right while joining a global network of young leaders. You'll receive guidance and training in financial health advocacy, create meaningful impact in your community, gain valuable experience in public engagement and leadership, and have the option to stay connected as an alumni mentor after completing the program.
Is there a cost to participate?
The program is completely FREE for selected participants! We believe in removing financial barriers to leadership opportunities and want to make this accessible to passionate young adults worldwide.
Have more questions?
Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our team at global@finmango.org
