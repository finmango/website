Affordability Lab

Exploring the intersection of AI, data, and financial health. We analyze affordability challenges facing young adults and develop solutions that matter.

Research Focus

What We Study

Tackling the biggest affordability barriers facing young adults.

🏠

Housing Costs

From rent burden to homeownership gaps, we analyze what keeps housing out of reach.

🚗

Transportation

Car dependency, auto loan burdens, and mobility equity across communities.

🥗

Food Security

Understanding food deserts, grocery costs, and nutrition access barriers.

💊

Healthcare Access

Insurance gaps, prescription costs, and medical debt affecting young adults.

📚

Student Debt

The growing crisis of education costs and its impact on financial futures.

💼

Income Inequality

Wage stagnation, gig economy challenges, and the wealth gap.
