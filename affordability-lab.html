How It Works

AI + Your Brain = Real Solutions

We're not teaching AI literacy. We're solving real problems. Here's how our quarterly challenges work.

1

Pick a Challenge

Each quarter, we focus on one systemic affordability barrier: housing, healthcare, education, food, or transportation.

2

Use AI to Research

Use free AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) as a thinking partner to explore solutions, not just get answers.

3

Submit Your Solution

Share what you discovered: the problem, your research process, the solution, and real results or projections.

4

Win & Help Others

Best solutions win $500. All solutions are shared publicly so the entire community can implement them.

Problem Solver Canvas

Build Your Solution

Use this interactive tool to develop your solution using AI as a thinking partner. We'll guide you through the process.

üß† Your AI-Powered Solution Canvas

Answer each step thoughtfully. AI prompts will help guide your research.

1

Define Your Specific Problem

Be specific. Include numbers, location, and constraints.

Try these AI prompts:
  • "What are 10 unconventional housing options that cost under $800/month in [your city]?"
  • "What percentage of income should go toward housing? What are the financial risks of paying more?"
  • "What are creative housing arrangements used in other countries that could work in the US?"
2

What Did You Discover Using AI?

Document what you learned and which AI tools you used.

Try these AI prompts:
  • "What government assistance programs exist for housing in [your city] for someone earning $[income]?"
  • "How do I calculate the true cost of different housing options including utilities, commute, etc.?"
  • "What questions should I ask when evaluating alternative housing arrangements for safety and legitimacy?"
3

Your Solution & Action Plan

Include specific steps and measurable outcomes (money saved, time saved, etc.).

Try these AI prompts:
  • "What are the potential risks of [your solution] and how can I mitigate them?"
  • "Create a checklist for implementing [your solution] step-by-step"
  • "What are signs that [your solution] is legitimate vs. a scam?"
4

Brain Check: How Did AI Help?

Reflect: Did you learn something new? Can you replicate this without AI next time?
