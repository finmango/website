The Poverty Premium

Why "drive till you qualify" is a trap.

Depreciation

The Silent Killer

Every mile you drive costs about $0.67 (IRS rate) in gas, wear, and tear. A 60-mile daily commute costs over $10,000 a year in vehicle expenses.

Time Value

Lost Life

Commuting 2 hours a day is 500 hours a year. That's 20 full days of your life spent in a car, unpaid and stressed.

Risk

One Breakdown Away

When you rely on a car to get to work, a breakdown means lost wages or job loss. Living closer offers transit, biking, or walking options.
